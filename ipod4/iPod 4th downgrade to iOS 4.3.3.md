iPod 4th downgrade to iOS 4.3.3

The latest ios is 6.1.6 for ipod 4, let's go for 4.3.3.

It's unpossible to downgrade without blobs and no OTA blobs available for ipod 4. So we need to use the custom IPSW provided by Geekgrade.

It's better to do this in win7 or older Mac. I choose vmare in Win11.

Download the itunes 11.0 for flashing the old firmware. use Shift key to choose the Geekgrade custom firmware.

After restored, you will get into Recovery mode, take it to DFU mode, we will need redsn0w to jailbreak and boot. Please use 0.9.15b3 for redsn0w.

Choose "Extras" -->"Select IPSW"-->Choose the restore official IPSW. (You will need this in every later step). Go back and Click "Jailbreak"

After the process of the pineapple, you will get into the Recovery mode again, so let's repeat the pervious steps again but not to choose Jailbreak, this time we choose "Extra"-->"Just boot"

If it stuck in boot, just press Top and home to reset and repeat the boot step again. You will need this process everytime you reboot the device.
